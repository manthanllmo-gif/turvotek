Product categories | Manufacturing: ACDB, DCDB  Trading: Solar Panels, Inverters, Cables, Earthing Systems, Lightning Arrestors, Structures and BoS.
Product descriptions | PRODUCT DESCRIPTIONS AC Distribution Box (ACDB) Turvotek AC Distribution Boxes (ACDB) are designed to provide safe and efficient power distribution between solar inverters and the utility grid or electrical loads. Manufactured using high-quality electrical components, our ACDBs offer protection against overloads, short circuits, voltage surges, and electrical faults. Suitable for residential, commercial, industrial, and utility-scale solar installations, our ACDBs ensure reliable performance, enhanced safety, and long service life. Key Features • High-quality MCCBs, MCBs & SPDs • Weatherproof and durable enclosures • Customized designs available • Easy installation and maintenance • Compliant with industry standards  DC Distribution Box (DCDB) Our DC Distribution Boxes (DCDB) provide critical protection and isolation between solar PV modules and inverters. Designed to withstand harsh environmental conditions, these boxes incorporate DC isolators, fuse protection, surge protection devices, and monitoring arrangements to maximize system safety and performance. Key Features • Efficient string protection and isolation • UV-resistant enclosure options • Enhanced surge protection • Suitable for rooftop and ground-mounted systems • Reliable long-term performance  Solar Panels (PV Modules) We supply high-efficiency solar photovoltaic modules from leading manufacturers, suitable for residential, commercial, industrial, and utility-scale projects. Available in Mono PERC, TOPCon, and Bifacial technologies, our solar panels deliver superior energy generation, durability, and performance under diverse environmental conditions. Key Features • High conversion efficiency • Long operational life • Excellent low-light performance • Robust weather resistance • Manufacturer-backed warranties  Solar Inverters Turvotek offers advanced grid-tied, hybrid, and off-grid solar inverters that efficiently convert DC power generated by solar panels into usable AC electricity. Equipped with intelligent monitoring systems and advanced protection features, our inverters maximize energy yield and ensure reliable system operation. Key Features • High conversion efficiency • MPPT technology for maximum power output • Remote monitoring capability • Advanced protection functions • Suitable for residential to utility-scale applications  Solar Cables & Connectors Our solar cables and connectors ensure safe, efficient, and reliable transmission of electrical power throughout the solar installation. Designed specifically for outdoor solar applications, these products offer excellent resistance to UV radiation, moisture, temperature fluctuations, and environmental stress. Key Features • UV and weather resistant • Low transmission losses • High durability and flexibility • International quality standards compliance • Long service life  Earthing Systems Turvotek provides comprehensive earthing solutions to protect personnel and equipment from electrical faults and lightning-induced surges. Our earthing systems are designed to ensure safe dissipation of fault currents and compliance with electrical safety standards. Key Features • Low earth resistance values • Corrosion-resistant materials • Easy installation • Long operational life • Enhanced safety and equipment protection  Lightning Arrestors Our lightning protection systems are designed to safeguard solar installations and electrical infrastructure from lightning strikes and transient voltage surges. By safely diverting surge currents to the ground, they protect valuable equipment and ensure uninterrupted operation. Key Features • Effective surge protection • Durable construction • Compliance with safety standards • Suitable for all solar project types • Long-term reliability  Solar Mounting Structures Turvotek manufactures and supplies robust solar mounting structures engineered for rooftop, elevated, and ground-mounted solar power plants. Constructed from galvanized steel or aluminium, our structures provide superior strength, corrosion resistance, and long-term durability. Key Features • Customized design options • Corrosion-resistant finish • High structural strength • Easy installation • Designed for Indian weather conditions  Balance of System (BoS) Components We offer a complete range of Balance of System (BoS) components required for efficient and reliable solar power plant installation. Our BoS portfolio includes connectors, junction boxes, hardware, fasteners, monitoring systems, cable management accessories, and other critical electrical and mechanical components. Key Features • Complete project support solutions • Quality-assured products • Compatibility with leading solar brands • Reliable performance • Cost-effective procurement
